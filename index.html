Common questions

Things people ask on the call.

Why not WordPress or a template?

Templates look like the next 10,000 sites built from the same template, which makes you forgettable when a customer is comparing five tabs. WordPress can be useful, but for focused service sites it often adds plugin bloat, security chores, and maintenance for things that should just work.

How long will my site take?

Most projects ship in 2-4 weeks from kick-off. Bigger scopes with CMS, multilingual pages, or integrations can take 4-6. I give you the timeline in the proposal before we start.

What about hosting and maintenance?

Hosting on Vercel or Netlify is usually free or a few dollars a month. I set it up under your account so you own it. Maintenance is optional; most clients only need occasional changes after launch.

Can I edit content myself?

Yes. Growth can include a lightweight CMS so you can edit copy, swap images, and add posts without touching code. Essential is best when the site will stay fairly stable.

Do I own the code?

Completely. The full source goes to your GitHub at handoff. You can take it to any developer, move hosts, or do whatever you want with it.

What if I need changes after launch?

The Growth tier includes a month of post-launch tweaks. After that, changes are hourly or I can stay on a small retainer. Either way, you'll get a rate before I start.
