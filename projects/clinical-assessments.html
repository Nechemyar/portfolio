Clinical Assessments

A calm, considered editorial site for a psychology practice — built to earn trust before the first call.

Client Clinical Assessments Ltd
Scope Brand site, design + build
Stack Hand-coded · Vite
Timeline 2 weeks
Visit the live site →

The brief.

Clinical Assessments came in with a templated site that treated a serious practice like any other service business — stock imagery, generic copy, and a contact form buried three clicks deep. Parents considering an assessment for their child were bouncing before they got to the practitioner's name.

We rebuilt from scratch: a calm editorial structure, considered typography, real photography of the practice, and a contact flow that puts the practitioner first. Every component hand-coded for speed and accessibility, PageSpeed 90+ across mobile and desktop.
